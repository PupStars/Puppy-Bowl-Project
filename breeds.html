Breeds

  • Labrador Retriever

    Labrador Retrievers are friendly and outgoing dogs known for their ability to work and companionship.

  • French Bulldog

    French Bulldogs are known for their bat-like ears and muscular, compact bodies. They are good companion dogs with a gentle nature.

  • Golden Retriever

    Golden Retrievers are friendly, intelligent, and devoted dogs. They are known for their ability to retrieve game undamaged due to their soft mouth.

  • Beagle

    Beagles are known for their excellent sense of smell and tracking instinct. They are friendly and great with kids, making them a wonderful family pet.

  • Poodle

    Poodles are known for their intelligence and ease of training. They come in three sizes, all of which belong to the same breed. Poodles have a hypoallergenic coat which requires regular grooming.
